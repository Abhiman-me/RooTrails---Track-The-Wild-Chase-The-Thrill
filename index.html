Our Mission

With RootRails, safaris in Sri Lanka become smarter and more meaningful. Our animal tracking technology helps travelers experience wildlife in its natural habitat while ensuring safety and supporting conservation. It's about turning every journey into an authentic adventure that also protects the wilderness.

RootRails also makes tourism transparent and scam-free. Business owners can list their services directly on the app, so travelers connect with verified experiences without middlemen or hidden costs. This creates a trustworthy platform where visitors explore with confidence, and local businesses grow fairly.
